Rounds & matches

Each round is Individual (own ball, group with anyone — personal board only), Pairs match play (Better Ball / Aggregate, standing or swapped), or Singles. Enter each player's gross; low net wins match-play — 1 point, ½ if tied.

▪ The team cup is decided by the match-play rounds — pairs = two duo matches, singles = four 1‑v‑1s, each match a point; most points wins, an even split retains. Individual rounds let everyone play their own ball in any group and count only toward the personal net leaderboard. Better Ball takes a duo's lower net; Aggregate adds both.
